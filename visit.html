Top Places to Visit in Edinburgh

Discover the rich culture and history of Edinburgh through its iconic landmarks and unforgettable experiences. Explore ancient fortresses, stroll historic streets, and immerse yourself in Scotland’s vibrant atmosphere.

Edinburgh Castle

Perched atop Castle Rock, Edinburgh Castle offers sweeping views, storied halls, and a deep dive into Scotland’s regal history.

Learn More
The Royal Mile

This historic stretch connects the castle with Holyrood Palace and is lined with fascinating shops, historic landmarks, and vibrant street performances.

Discover More
Arthur's Seat

A majestic ancient volcano that offers a rewarding hike and panoramic views of Edinburgh’s skyline – a haven for nature lovers.

Explore More
National Museum of Scotland

Uncover Scotland’s rich history and diverse culture through engaging exhibits and interactive displays that captivate all ages.

Visit Museum

Essential Experiences

  • Savor traditional Scottish dishes at local pubs and restaurants.
  • Wander through the charming streets of the Grassmarket area.
  • Join a ghost tour to explore Edinburgh's mysterious past.
  • Relax in the serene Princes Street Gardens.
  • Experience live music and cultural festivals throughout the year.
